Slow-cooker Chili with Beans

A mouth watering and classic recipe. Great topped with sour cream and chopped green onions!

Description

This slow cooker chili is packed with beans, veggies, and spices. The crockpot is a great way to make this beefy chili.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 large diced onion
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp cumin powder
  • 2 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 16 oz. can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 30 oz of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 10 oz of diced tomatoes with green chilis, with juice
  • 30 oz tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Sautee beef in large skillet until it releases fat (4-5 minutes), breaking it up with a spatula
  2. Add onion to the skillet and sautee until tender. (4-5 minutes)
  3. Add minced garlic, and seasonings: cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook for 30 seconds, stirring constantly. Transfer to slow cooker.
  4. Add remaining ingredients to slow cooker: rinsed beans, diced tomatoes with chilis, and tomato sauce. Stir to combine and cook on high for 3-4 hours, or low for 6-8 hours. Season to taste if desired and serve warm.
  5. Top with any ingredients you wish. Some great choices: Shredded cheese, green or white onions, avocado, tortilla strips, sour cream.
